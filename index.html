How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one! Your board will start to shrink if you fall behind the other player!

Created by Andy Zhang and Jean-Marc Goepfert. Based on 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli, 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
